Privacy

word chain snake runs in the browser as a static web game. The game itself does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

If the optional ranking server is enabled, submitted scores may include the display name typed by the player, score, board size, filled-cell count, turn count, finish type, and submission time. Do not enter a real name if you do not want it shown on the leaderboard.

If Cloudflare Web Analytics is enabled, Cloudflare may process page visit and performance information to provide aggregate traffic reports. The game may also send aggregate gameplay events such as game start, finish type, board size, language, and result sharing count to the ranking server. These gameplay events do not include the words typed on the board.

If additional analytics or hosting-provider tools are added later, those third-party services may collect cookies, device information, IP addresses, or usage data according to their own policies. Update this page before enabling those services in production.

If Google AdSense is enabled, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites. Users can manage personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings.
